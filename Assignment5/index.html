Recombination Museum
Computation, Culture & Society 2026 · Prof. James A. Evans · UChicago

This is not a memory palace of the syllabus. It is a machine for re-sorting it. Inspired by Vannevar Bush's memex and Douglas Engelbart's augmented intelligence program, this tool helps you construct associative trails across course materials—noticing connections the weekly calendar conceals.

Lenses reorganize the same readings through different conceptual frames. Rooms are cross-week thematic groupings. Trails are associative paths through the course—you can build your own. Contradictions pairs readings in productive tension. Ghosts shows recurring themes haunting the course.
